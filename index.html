ABOUT US
Our Vessels Georgios Mproufas and Georgios Mproufas II were made in 1997 and 2008 respectively by Mr. Kroustallis S. Georgios. They are active since then in route Peiraeus-Paloukia(Salamis) with intermediate stop at Kamatero(Salamis) dock. They are 25 meters long with a 3-man crew including a captain, an engineer and a sailor. Annually from 21 August until 24 August their route includes M. Peuko- Faneromeni easing the access of the pilmgrims to the feast of Holy Monasteri of Faneromeni. Every Summer our vessels are chartered for trips around Aigina, Agia Marina, Aigina, Poros, Ydra, Spetses and Agkistri. In Summer 2016 E/G Georgios Mproufas II was chartered for trips around Ios, Sikinos and Folegandros.
